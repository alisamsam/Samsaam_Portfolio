  • Duration: S7 (2024) to S8 (2025)
  • Innovation: Automated medication reminder and tracking system

CAD Modeling - Jet Turbine Mechanism (2022)

Comprehensive 3D modeling project showcasing advanced CAD skills and understanding of complex mechanical systems.

Project Scope

  • Software Used: Professional CAD software (AutoCAD, SolidWorks)
  • Components: Complete jet turbine assembly with multiple parts
  • Documentation: Detailed assembly process documentation (PDF)
  • Presentation: Video demonstration of 3D model (MP4)

Technical Details

The project involved creating detailed 3D models of all turbine components, including compressor blades, combustion chamber, turbine shaft, and housing. Each part was designed with proper tolerances and assembly constraints.

The final deliverable included engineering drawings, bill of materials, and an animated assembly sequence showing how all components fit together. This project demonstrated proficiency in:

  • 3D modeling and parametric design
  • Assembly constraints and mate relationships
  • Technical documentation and drawing standards
  • Visualization and presentation techniques
